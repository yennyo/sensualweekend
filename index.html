About

Three Days of Parties!

A dance festival where the focus is on social dancing.
Top workshops, parties, shows and competitions.
The parties will finish after sunrise and workshops only start in the afternoon, so you'll get enough sleep.
For the full schedule and more details, check out the Facebook event page!

Highlights

  • Fri Aug 15 - Sun Aug 17

    Parties until 5 am

    20:00 - 5:00

    Every night: shows, parties, animations, and all dancing you can do until 5 am.

  • Saturday Aug. 16

    Bachatart Norway

    22:00

    Bachatart is a world competition in bachata. The participants will be presenting their choreographies and the winner will represent Norway in the Bachatart World competition.

  • Fri Aug 15 - Sun Aug 17

    Top workshops

    12:00 - 17:30

    Top quality workshops by amazing instructors from Spain, France and the Netherlands. Check out the full workshop schedule on the Facebook event page.
