About

Three Days of Party!

15 - 17 August 2014, Oslo, Norway

A new concept for dance festival where the focus is on social dancing.

Top workshops, parties shows and competitions.

The parties will finish after sunrise and you will not miss workshops by getting enough sleep.

Highlights

  • Friday Aug. 15

    Party until 5 am

    20:00 - 5:00

    Shows, party animations and all dancing you can do until 5 am.

  • Saturday Aug. 16

    Bachatarte Norway

    22:00

    Bachatarte is a world competition in bachata. The participants will be presenting their choreographies and the winner will represent Norway in the Bachatart World 2014.

  • Sunday Aug. 17

    Jack & Jill competition

    22:00

    Jack and Jill is a type of improvisation competition where everyone has fun dancing with random partners. There will be a Bachata competition and a Kizomba competition.
